# Lyniva User Manual 📱

Welcome to **Lyniva**, an innovative app designed to help you track your activities, eating habits, and well-being, all while interacting with a virtual character whose health directly mirrors yours!

---

## 🐣 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Account Creation & Login
- Open the application.
- Tap **Sign Up** to create a new account, or **Log In** if you already have one.
- You can create an account using your email and a secure password. Setup your basic profile on the initial landing screens.

### 1.2 Meet Your Virtual Pet/Avatar
Upon registering, you'll meet your digital companion. The health, mood, and evolution of your companion depend entirely on the choices *you* make. When you eat well and exercise, your companion thrives!

---

## 🗺️ 2. Core Features

### 2.1 Geolocation & Activity Tracking
- Head to the **Activity/Workout** tab.
- Tap **Start Activity** and choose your workout mode (Running, Walking, Cycling).
- The app utilizes real-time GPS tracking to map your route, record your distance, output, and duration.
- When you finish, tap **End Activity** to finalize and review your stats.

### 2.2 Food Logging & Image Recognition
- Want to track what you're eating? Navigate to the **Food Log**.
- Use the **Camera** to snap a photo of your meal.
- Lyniva’s built-in Image Recognition will try to identify the food and automatically log nutritional values (or prompt you to refine the entry).
- Logging healthy meals increases your virtual companion's vitality score.

### 2.3 Mood Check-ins
- Check in periodically with the **Mood** tracker.
- Describe how you’re feeling. The app’s underlying Sentiment Analysis will detect stress, positivity, or fatigue, providing actionable wellness tips.

---

## 🧍‍♀️ 3. Caring for Your Virtual Companion

Your companion is a visual representation of your well-being.
- **Thriving:** Achieved by completing daily activity goals, eating balanced meals, and consistent mood wellness.
- **Sluggish/Sad:** Occurs when you miss activities for several days or register poor health indicators in a row.
- **Interaction:** Tap your character in the Main Hub to check its current stats (which correspond directly to your recorded wellness levels).

---

## 👥 4. Social Network and Leaderboards

- Navigate to the **Community** tab or **Feed**.
- Share your workouts and meals with friends or the broader network.
- Give a thumbs up (or "kudos") and comment on others' healthy choices.
- Join the **Leaderboards** by earning achievements for consecutive exercise days, highest step counts, etc. Let friendly competition drive your wellness! 

---

## ⚙️ 5. Profile & Settings

- Adjust your account settings from your **Profile** page.
- View your history, overall stats, connected friends, and attained achievements.
- Adjust notification settings (e.g., set a reminder for your daily mood check-in or workout).

---

## ❓ FAQ & Troubleshooting

* **My GPS activity isn't tracking properly.**
  Ensure that you have granted Lyniva location permissions (set to "Always Allow" or "Allow while using app" in your phone's settings).
* **The app did not recognize my food photo correctly.**
  Make sure there corresponds lighting or manually adjust the ingredients from the selection menu post-snap.

Enjoy building a better you—and a happier virtual companion—with Lyniva!
